Projects

  • “RF Controlled Metal Detection ROBOT” - For Assisting Bomb Detection And Rescue Team.

    Description: Metal detection using RF Controlled Robot is proposed in this project. The robot has the camera in it which captures a live video and send to the rescue person. It has the PIR sensor to sense the human and an ultrasonic sensor to detect the obstacles. The video of the alive human in the rescue scenario is displayed on the Laptop which is held by the rescue person. This can be used to detect Terrorists hidden inside the building and also alive humans from buildings that have been collapsed due to Natural calamities like Earthquakes.
